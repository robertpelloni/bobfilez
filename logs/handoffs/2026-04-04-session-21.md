## Key architectural clarification
BobUI is a **Qt fork**, so the correct integration is not to replace every `Qt6::*` target with a fictional `BobUI::*` namespace.

Instead:
1. build/install BobUI
2. expose its generated Qt6 package configs
3. let bobfilez resolve normal `find_package(Qt6 ...)` calls from BobUI
